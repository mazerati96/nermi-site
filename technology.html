Three dimensions, three autonomic branches.

NerMI decomposes the PPG signal into lawful physiological components, mapping directly onto parasympathetic, α-sympathetic, and β-sympathetic activity—no guessing, no generic "stress scores."

Single PPG input → multi-dimensional output

Dimension I: Valence

Parasympathetic (PNS)

Tracks comfort vs. discomfort through vagal-linked patterns in low-frequency PPG dynamics, acting as a continuous "pleasantness" signal rather than post-hoc sentiment.

Aligned to EMG (zygomaticus–corrugator)
Seconds → weeks

Dimension II: Motivation

α-Sympathetic (α-SNS)

Captures approach-vs-avoidance by extracting mid-frequency oscillations tied to effort, readiness, and behavioral drive—what moves a person toward or away from goals.

Persistent baseline activation
Minutes → weeks

Dimension III: Activation

β-Sympathetic (β-SNS)

Measures autonomic arousal from heart-rate variability in the ultra-high-frequency band, marking the body's readiness for action—whether excited or stressed.

Short-term autonomic bursts
Seconds → hours

One measurement layer across timescales.

An extended Kalman filter tracks band-limited oscillations within the PPG stream, enabling emotion, state, and trait inference using the same model—no re-training, no hidden modes.

On-device · C-optimized · Cloud-optional

Real-time EKF dynamics

NerMI's EKF tracks oscillatory components at predefined frequencies (VLF, LF, HF, UHF), converting standard PPG into a continuous map of autonomic behavior.

Seconds

Emotion: heartbeat-level updates in Valence, Motivation, and Activation for moment-to-moment changes.

Minutes–hours

States: work sessions, interactions, recovery windows, and sleep stages become structured patterns.

Days–weeks

Traits: repeated behavioral signatures stabilize into temperament and personality profiles.

Why it works when black boxes don't

Instead of throwing data at a deep net, NerMI encodes the physiology directly. That keeps the logic visible and the computation light.

Lawful physiology

Each dimension maps to a known autonomic mechanism, not a hidden layer.

Transparent math

EKF + frequency decomposition can be inspected, audited, and replicated.

Hardware-agnostic

If a device exposes PPG with IBI and pulse volume, NerMI can run on it.

Predictable cost

Designed for constrained wearables, not server farms.
