What is NerMI™?

Not another "stress score." A way of seeing how people actually shift, engage, and react.

NerMI™ is a real-time emotion layer that estimates physiology and behavior. It doesn't replace your product. It gives your product a way to measure their emotions, cognitive-behavioral changes, and personality.

The details live in our technology pages. The short version: NerMI™ estimates structured emotional patterns from continuous signals and makes them usable.
